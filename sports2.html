SPORTS AND CULTURE

Eligibility

1) The applicant must be a Serving or Retired Sportsperson (including sports doctors, sports psychologists, sports mentor, physiotherapists, masseurs, coaches, support personnel, umpires, referees, match officials)
2) The applicant must have represented India at a National or International Level sports competition.
3) The Annual income of self / family of the applicant (from all sources) must be less than Rs. 4 LPA.
4) The applicant must qualify as one of the following -
● Injured during the period of their training for competitions and also during the competitions.
● Disabled as an after effect of their strenuous training or otherwise.
● You or your dependents are living in and their dependents in indigent circumstances.
● Dependent of a deceased sportsperson

Application Process

Offline:
Step 1: An application for financial assistance from the Fund shall be addressed to the Member-Secretary of the Executive Committee in the prescribed Performa given in the scheme guidelines.
The filled in application is to be sent to the following address:
Member - Secretary
Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS)
Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports
Shastri Bhawan, New Delhi - 110001

Documents Required

1) Applicant’s Photo.
2) Proof of Age / DOB.
3) Proof of Achievements / Participation in National or International Level sports events.
4) Bank Details
5) Annual Income of the applicant (from all sources)
6) Declaration (signed by the applicant and two witnesses).
