SPORTS AND CULTURE

Eligibility

1) The applicant must be a sportsperson.
2) The applicant must have won a Gold, Silver, or Bronze medal in an international competition, i.e. Olympic Games, World Cup / World Championships in Olympics and Asian Games disciplines, Asian Games, Commonwealth Games and Paralympic Games.

Application Process

Offline:
Step 1: Take print of the prescribed proforma of the application form and the Mandate Form given in Annexure-I and Annexure-II of the scheme guidelines available at Link
Step 2: The duly filled application along with the required documents are to be sent to the address of the concerned National Sports Federation for recommendation.

Documents Required

1) Proof of Age / DOB
2) Aadhaar
3) Bank Details
4) Documents that are a proof of winning Gold, Silver or Bronze medal(s) in international competition
