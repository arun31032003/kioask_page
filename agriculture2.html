AGRICULTURE RURAL AND ENVIRONMENT

Eligibility

RAll landholding farmers' families, which have cultivable land holding in their names are eligible to get benefit under the scheme.

Application Process

Online-via CSCs
1) Following are the prerequisites for the enrollment process:
● Aadhaar Card
● Landholding paper
● Saving bank account
2) The VLE will fill the complete details of Farmer registration details like, State, district, subdistrict block, and village, key-in the Aadhaar number, Name of beneficiary, category, Bank detail, Land Registration ID and Date of birth as printed on aadhaar card for authentication..
3) The VLE will fill the Land details like Survey/ Kahta No., Khasra no. and area of land as per mention in land holding papers.
4) Upload the supporting document like Land, Aadhar, Bank passbook.
5) Self declaration accept and save the application form.
6) After save the application form make payment through CSC ID.
7) Check the Beneficiary status through Aadhar number

Documents Required

1) Aadhaar Card
2) Landholding papers
3) Savings Bank Account.
